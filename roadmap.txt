
================== DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP FOR BUDOSTACK ===================

 This is a public development roadmap for BUDOSTACK, mainly composed out
 of my personal interests using BUDOSTACK to my other hobby projects,
 such as the BUDOSTACK MS-DOS Application development using Borland C++
 Version 3.1 environment with Dosbox emulating MS-DOS 5.0.
